Rooms for pleasent dreams

Our spacious rooms and suites are thoughtfully decorated with local art and comfortable furnishings, ensuring a cozy haven after a day of exploring. Each room features plush bedding, complimentary Wi-Fi, and private balconies where you can enjoy the breathtaking sunsets that paint the sky in hues of gold and lavender. For those seeking an extra touch of luxury, our signature suites come with whirlpool tubs and fireplaces, perfect for a romantic escape.

Our gym facilities

Our state-of-the-art gym is equipped with the latest cardio and strength-training machines, ensuring a comprehensive workout experience for all fitness levels. With panoramic views of the city and natural light flooding the space, you’ll feel energized and motivated. Complimentary towels, water stations, and personal trainers are available to assist with personalized workout plans.

Spa and beauty lab

Escape into our serene Spa and Beauty Lab, where luxury meets relaxation. Indulge in a range of treatments from rejuvenating massages to revitalizing facials, all designed to refresh your body and mind. Our expert therapists use premium products to deliver bespoke services that cater to your individual needs, leaving you feeling pampered and renewed.

State of art swimming pool

Dive into our stunning swimming pool, a serene oasis designed for relaxation and leisure. Surrounded by elegant loungers and lush greenery, the heated pool features underwater lighting, perfect for both day and night swims. Whether you’re looking to swim laps or simply unwind, our pool provides the perfect backdrop.

Enticing food and beverage

Discover a culinary journey that delights the senses at our on-site restaurants and bars. From gourmet breakfasts to innovative fine dining, our menus are curated by expert chefs who blend local flavors with international flair. Enjoy handcrafted cocktails at our rooftop bar with panoramic city views, or savor a quiet dinner with a carefully selected wine pairing.
